DEFENSE

A SITE: DEFENSE_01

Standard A Site defense for A Main.

Camera

» Camera high up so it can check deep into A main to give early info.

Trapwires

» First wire for attackers entering site, second for anyone positioning towards the lever.

Cages

» First cage is used to peek from left side of dice, second to protect while repositioning. Instead of the oneway lineup you can use a simple cage on the ground just outside of A main entrance.

Playstyle

» Check cameras for attackers in A Main, then peek from left side of dice with A Main oneway, when exposed use second cage reposition and adapt.

A SITE: DEFENSE_02

Split A Site defense, A Main and Tree areas.

Camera

» Lower camera to check into tree, but cannot see inside A Main.

A Main

» Standard wire and cage to stop pushes.

Tree

» Wire and oneway to shoot from site.

Playstyle

» Check camera to see if attackers are coming from Tree, activate oneway and shoot behind the cages, beware of A Main, if there attackers there you can use the other cage to protect yourself.

A SITE: TREE

One way from garden to Tree, plus standard A Main defense for teammates..

A Site

» Standard wire and cage for A Main.

Garden

» High camera can check the feet of attackers coming from catwalk cubby into tree, very hard to spot.

Tree

» Wire and oneway to shoot from garden.

Playstyle

» Check camera, when spotting legs of enemies activate oneway and shoot, standing in the platform upstairs enemies from catwalk cubby cannot see you.
